About Our Remodeling & Renovation Projects

Avey Construction is a Toronto company specializing in bathroom remodeling, kitchen remodeling, basement finishing, and condo renovation. With a strong focus on quality and customer satisfaction, we strive to transform your spaces into stunning and functional areas that exceed your expectations. Our team of skilled professionals possesses extensive experience in the construction industry, ensuring that every project is executed with precision and attention to detail. From design conceptualization to completion, we work closely with you to understand your unique vision and bring it to life.


Whether you’re looking to renovate your bathroom, upgrade your kitchen, finish your basement, or make-over your condo, our team is equipped with the expertise and resources to deliver exceptional results. We are dedicated to providing personalized service, utilizing top-notch materials, and employing the latest techniques, like 3D modeling, to design and create spaces that enhance both aesthetics and functionality. Choose Avey Construction for your remodeling and renovation needs, and let us help you turn your dream space into a reality. Contact us today to get started on your next home renovation project.


About Avey's 2 Year Renovation Warranty

All of our renovation projects come with a two-year construction warranty, which includes anything that we will have built in your home. If any problems arise with our installations during these 2 years, such as leakages, cracks on paint or grout lines, or anything else related to our renovation project, we will come and fix it - for free! Beside our two-year construction warranty, all the materials in our catalogue come with their own warranty, usually starting from 10 years.
