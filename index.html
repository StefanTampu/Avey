9 Steps To Your Dream Home Upgrade

Keeping Your Bath & Kitchen Renovation Projects Hassle-Free

Step 1: Contact

Please submit a contact form below or give us a call. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

Step 2: Schedule Site Visit

Please let us know when you would be available for a home visit so that we may accomodate you.

Step 3: Site Visit

We will take measurements, make an exact floor plan, and confirm all of the materials that you want, either from our catalogue or from elsewhere.

Step 4: Final Quote

Based on the information we've collected from you, we will create a final, detailed quote and material list for the project.

Step 5: 3D Design

After you've confirmed the quote, we will create a 3D design for you based on the floor plan and the materials that you've chosen, and...

Step 6: Final Confirmation

Once everything is confirmed, we will order materials for the projects.

Step 7: Project Scheduling

This is based on the ETA of the materials, to ensure a smooth start to your home renovation project.

Step 8: Deliveries

In the week prior to the start of your project, we will deliver all the materials (usually on Wednesday or Thursday), and on Saturday we will bring the garbage bin to your home.

Step 9: Project Start & Finish

Projects start on Mondays. Depending on the size of the project, it usually takes 7 business days to complete one bathroom renovation project and 15 business days for kitchen renovation projects.
