Remodeling And Renovation Services In Toronto And Southern Ontario

Bathroom Renovation

Transform your bathroom into a sanctuary of relaxation and functionality with our comprehensive bathroom remodeling services. From updating fixtures and cabinetry to creating a spa-like atmosphere, we pay attention to every detail to ensure a stunning and practical result. Our team of experts will work with you to design a customized space that reflects your style and meets your specific needs, delivering a bathroom that is both beautiful and functional.


Kitchen Renovation

Revitalize your kitchen and enhance its functionality with our professional kitchen remodeling services. Whether you're looking to update the cabinets, countertops, appliances, or overall layout, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life. Our skilled team will guide you through the process, offering innovative design solutions and using high-quality materials to create a kitchen that becomes the heart of your home.


Basement Finish

Maximize the potential of your basement by utilizing our basement finishing services. We will transform your unfinished or underutilized space into a functional and inviting area that suits your needs. Whether you envision a home theater, a guest suite, a game room, or a home office, we will work closely with you to design and construct a finished basement that adds value and additional living space to your home.

Our team deals with all city permit procedures needed for basement finishes and takes care of engineering drawings, which are often a main source of headaches for homeowners. Our engineers will take measurements at your home to make a drawing and then apply for city permit on your behalf.


3D Renovation Modeling

Our 3D modeling is available for our bathroom and kitchen renovation services. We offer highly realistic 3D designs (see below) based on your bathrooms or kitchens' floor plan, measurement and materials that you choose. You will be able to see your future, upgraded bathroom and kitchen even before we start the project, as the final product looks virtually identical to the 3D models.


Condo Renovation

Elevate your condo living experience with our condo renovation services. We understand the unique challenges and considerations that come with renovating a condo, and our team is experienced in delivering successful condo renovation projects. From updating the flooring, lighting, and fixtures to reconfiguring the layout, we will help you optimize your space and create a stylish and functional condo that suits your lifestyle. Our team will get in touch with condo management and provide everything needed to get permission for the project.
